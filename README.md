# ClearMind

ClearMind is a web platform designed to make psychological support more accessible, personalized, and secure. Users complete an adaptive questionnaire to better understand their needs and receive tailored recommendations for licensed psychologists — removing the friction that often stands between people and mental health care.

____

## Features

- **Interactive intake questionnaire** — guides users through a structured self-assessment to surface the right therapeutic match for their situation
- **Psychologist directory** — browsable profiles with specializations, approaches, and availability
- **Appointment booking & session management** — end-to-end scheduling from first contact to ongoing care
- **Admin panel** — platform management interface for overseeing users, therapists, and overall system activity
- **Therapist panel** — dedicated dashboard for licensed psychologists to manage their profile, appointments, and client sessions
- **Secure & confidential data handling** — built with privacy as a baseline, not an afterthought
- **Accessible, intuitive design** — clear interface suited to users who may be approaching mental health support for the first time
